Play Frogger

Instructions

  • Move using A(left), W(up), S(down), D(right) keys
  • Attempt to reach the end of the map
  • Avoid getting hit by the cars
  • Avoid drowning in water
  • Getting hit by a car or drowning will cost you a life
  • Use the logs to safely cross the water
  • Reaching the edge of the map will grant you +100 points
  • Reaching the edge of the map will increase level speed
